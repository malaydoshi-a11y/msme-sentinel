SUPPORTING RIGOR METRICS
Why balanced accuracy, not raw accuracy: on this imbalanced default dataset, a model that predicts "no default" for every account can score 90%+ raw accuracy while being useless. Balanced accuracy (average of sensitivity and specificity) is still honestly "accuracy" — just measured correctly. AUC-ROC, KS-statistic, and Gini coefficient are the industry-standard credit-risk metrics that confirm this number reflects real discriminative power, not an artifact of class imbalance. Full reasoning: docs/metrics_note.md.
